About Us

CinemaPedia

CinemaPedia is a Community where you can find details of a film ,cast & crew.It is especially dedicated to moviebuffs.Connecting & educating people through the art of cinema .....!!!

“There is no end. There is no beginning. There is only the infinite passion of life. ”-Cinema!!!


Our Mission:

To Deliver details about movies, and also To inspire, inform and entertain through the universal language of cinema arts and to serve as a cultural and educational platform for all.


Our Vision:

To evolve as a Film-industry oriented, research based Website for Details information in various Categories of Film-industry with an ultimate objective of meeting users requirement and demand
